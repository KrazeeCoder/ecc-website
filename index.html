About us

Evergreen Chess Club is a 501(c)(3) organization dedicated to making chess accessible and impactful across the Greater Seattle area. We believe chess builds focus, community, and opportunity for all ages.

Our current leadership includes one officer: Nihanth Tatikonda — President.

  • Serving Bothell, Redmond, Bellevue, and Greater Seattle
  • USCF-rated and free NWSRS tournaments
  • Instruction for newcomers and returning players
  • Fundraising through chess for charitable causes

What we do

Teach chess

Beginner-friendly instruction, workshops, and community meetups to grow the game.

Run quality tournaments

We host USCF-rated events and free NWSRS events with clear sections and fair pairings.

Support charity

Proceeds from tournaments help support charitable initiatives in our community.
