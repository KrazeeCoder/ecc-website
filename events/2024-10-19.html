Evergreen Fall Chess Event — Oct 19, 2024

Our first official event brought together players from Bothell, Redmond, Bellevue, and beyond for a day of thoughtful games and community building. We hosted sections for different experience levels and awarded prizes thanks to generous supporters.

Highlights

  • Multiple sections for fair pairings
  • USCF-rated games, with a free NWSRS-friendly side section
  • Proceeds contributed to charitable initiatives
  • Great sportsmanship and community support
